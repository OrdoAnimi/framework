# Velocity Ecosystem Brand System

**ZenCloud Global Consultants**  
Classification: Internal — Design and Communications  
Version: 1.0 — May 2026

---

## Governing Principle

**Same shell. Different product content.**

Every site, product, and publication in the Velocity ecosystem shares one visual and structural shell. The shell is not negotiable. Product content — name, purpose, primary CTA, content sections — varies per product.

This is not a style guide. It is a system constraint.

## Brand Roles

### ZenCloud Global Consultants

ZenCloud is the advisory and consulting face of the ecosystem. It owns client relationships, delivers engagements, and carries professional accountability.

- Tone: authoritative, calm, direct, enterprise-grade
- Audience: SLT, enterprise architecture leaders, program directors, consultancy partners
- Not: tech-forward, product-led, academic

ZenCloud does not have a product catalogue. It has an engagement model and a trusted operating method (Velocity).

---

### Velocity Architecture Framework

Velocity is the decision-first operating model. It is the method, the certification, and the academy.

- Tone: precise, structured, practitioner-facing, confident
- Audience: architects, delivery leads, EA and SA practitioners, governance teams
- Not: marketing-heavy, jargon-dense, consultant-speak

Velocity is what practitioners use. ZenCloud is who clients call.

---

### VAF Agentic AI

VAF Agentic AI is the tool layer. It accelerates artefact production under practitioner direction.

- Tone: functional, capability-focused
- Audience: Velocity practitioners, technically capable users
- Not: consumer-facing, AI-hype language

Never position VAF Agentic AI as autonomous. It operates under practitioner accountability.

---

### VAF-SA

VAF-SA is the solution architecture practitioner method — the structured way to scope, design, and govern a solution within the Velocity model.

- Tone: methodical, practitioner-grade
- Audience: solution architects, technical leads
- Not: executive-facing

---

### PMO Portal

The PMO Portal is the delivery governance workspace — where program health, decision registers, and architecture status are tracked.

- Tone: operational, clear, status-oriented
- Audience: program directors, delivery leads, PMO functions
- Not: architecture-heavy

---

### StudioSix

StudioSix is the production studio. It makes things: books, podcasts, YouTube content, research papers, training modules, course content, and experimental digital products.

- Tone: creative, editorial, product-led
- Audience: practitioners, learners, general professional audience
- Not: enterprise-formal

StudioSix operates under ZenCloud's umbrella but with a distinct voice.

---

### StudioSix Labs

Labs is where personal research, experimental products, and trading systems live. It is clearly separated from commercial advisory and enterprise client work.

- Always labelled: "StudioSix Labs — personal research"
- Never co-branded with ZenCloud advisory or presented to enterprise clients
- Financial and trading content always carries appropriate disclaimers

## Logo Family Requirements

Each brand in the ecosystem requires:

1. **Primary lockup:** Wordmark + optional icon, full colour
2. **Reversed:** White on dark background
3. **Monochrome:** Single colour, for document headers and print
4. **Favicon / avatar:** Square crop, icon only, works at 32×32px

### Recommended Velocity Mark

A four-box grid mark — representing the four-box operating model (EA / BA / SA / PMO) — with the Velocity wordmark beneath or beside it.

The four-box mark is the ecosystem's visual signature. It communicates the model without explanation.

```
┌──┬──┐
│EA│BA│
├──┼──┤
│SA│PM│
└──┴──┘
  VELOCITY
```

Sub-brands (VAF Agentic AI, VAF-SA, PMO Portal) use the parent Velocity mark with a product label beneath.

ZenCloud uses its own separate mark — not derived from the Velocity grid.

StudioSix uses its own separate mark — creative, distinct from the enterprise visual of Velocity.

## Navigation Rules

**Top navigation** (all sites):

- Top-left: ecosystem or product logo
- Centre: primary section links (3–5 items maximum)
- Top-right: one primary CTA (button, not link)

**Left-side section navigation** (optional):

- Used on content-heavy pages (framework documentation, academy content, portal)
- Not used on marketing/landing pages
- Sticky on scroll

**Navigation rules:**

- No dropdown mega-menus
- Maximum depth: two levels
- Mobile: hamburger menu, same items
- Active state: Signal Orange underline or highlight

## CTA Rules

- One primary CTA per page — top right in header
- Secondary CTAs within page content — outlined button style
- Tertiary actions — text links
- No more than two CTAs visible in the hero at one time
- CTA labels: action verbs, specific ("Start with Velocity" not "Learn More")

## Do / Don't

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Velocity compresses the working patterns of enterprise architecture | Velocity replaces your architects |
| AI-assisted artefact production under practitioner accountability | AI does the architecture work for you |
| Decision-first, artefact-second | Paperwork-free architecture |
| ZenCloud advises enterprise clients | ZenCloud is a tech startup |
| StudioSix produces content and tools | StudioSix is a creative agency |
| VAF Agentic AI generates drafts for review | The AI makes the decisions |
| Same shell, different product content | Each site is built differently |
| One primary CTA per page | Multiple competing calls to action |
