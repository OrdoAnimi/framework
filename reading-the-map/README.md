**Author:** Phil Myint · Principal Architect · ZenCloud Global Consultants  
**Status:** Draft in progress — Chapters 1–20 complete  
**Companion to:** Velocity Architecture Framework (VAF)

---

## What this book is

A navigation guide. Not a certification manual. Not a practitioner handbook.

This book is for everyone in IT who needs to understand the architectural terrain — well enough to follow conversations, ask sensible questions, and know what is being decided and why — without sitting an exam or becoming an architect themselves.

The book that comes before the textbooks become relevant.

---

## Worked example

Every abstraction is grounded in **Meridian Health Partnership** — a fictional NHS trust, 14,000 staff, 180 sites, VMware licensing crisis, dual EPR, no cloud. Real conditions. Real constraints. Real decisions.

## Relationship to the VAF

*Reading the Map* is the literacy companion to the Velocity Architecture Framework. The VAF is the practitioner framework — how to do architecture well. This book is the on-ramp — what the territory looks like before you decide to go deeper. They are sequential, not competing.
