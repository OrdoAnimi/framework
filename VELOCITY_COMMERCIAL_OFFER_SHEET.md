# Velocity Commercial Offer Sheet

## Purpose

This sheet summarises packaged offers that partners, ZenCloud, or StudioSix can use to discuss Velocity-aligned work. Prices are intentionally placeholders.

## Offers

| Offer | Duration | Audience | Outputs | Price |
|---|---:|---|---|---|
| Executive Architecture FastStart | 5 days or 2-4 weeks | SLT, CIO, transformation lead | Executive architecture readout, decision map, risk view, 90-day plan | TBD |
| Architecture Decision Sprint | 4 weeks | EA, SA, PMO, delivery leaders | Decision-flow diagnostic, governance model, artefact set, execution recommendations | TBD |
| VAF-SA Engagement Diagnostic | 1-2 weeks | Solution architecture leads | SA maturity view, decision bottlenecks, guardrails, recommended artefacts | TBD |
| PMO Governance Alignment | 1-3 weeks | PMO, programme leads, EA | PMO/EA alignment model, RAID/decision linkage, governance cadence | TBD |
| Velocity AI Enablement | 1-2 weeks | Architecture and delivery teams | AI policy, safe workflow, draft/review controls, prompt/template pack | TBD |
| Advisory Retainer | Monthly | Executives and delivery leads | Ongoing architecture advice, decision review, governance support | TBD |
| Data/Security/Business Readiness Assessment | 1-3 weeks | CIO, CISO, data, business architecture leads | Readiness score, risks, decision map, action plan | TBD |

## Inclusions

- discovery and review sessions
- decision-first diagnostic
- architecture / delivery artefact recommendations
- executive summary
- practitioner review
- partner/client-safe outputs

## Exclusions

- production system implementation
- legal or regulatory sign-off
- unmanaged use of confidential data
- guaranteed delivery outcomes without client action
- final decisions made by AI
- enterprise platform deployment unless separately scoped

## Commercial Notes

- Partner-led engagements may be rebadged.
- Prices depend on scope, access, urgency, confidentiality, and delivery model.
- Fixed-fee offers require clear inputs and acceptance criteria.
- Retainers require weekly or fortnightly cadence.

## Data and AI Boundary

No confidential client data should be used in public tools. AI outputs are draft inputs to human-reviewed architecture work.
