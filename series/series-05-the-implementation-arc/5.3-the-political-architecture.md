# 5.3 — The Political Architecture

Air cover is not a favour. It is a structural precondition.

This article examines the political conditions required for a functioning decision system to survive organisational pressure.

It introduces:

- Air cover
- Mandate clarity
- Structural Courage
- Consequence design
- Decision Footprints
- Liability transfer reversal

The argument is direct: architecture without mandate collapses into advisory theatre.
