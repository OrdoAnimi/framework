# 5.1 — Reading the Room

Before you build anything, you need to know what you inherited.

Series 5 begins inside a real organisation carrying governance drift, undocumented delivery reality, ageing infrastructure, and a capability model disconnected from decision pressure.

The article introduces:

- Configuration Inheritance
- Decision Latency
- Baseline Integrity
- Authority Clarity
- Deviation Pattern analysis
- Measurement model diagnostics

The implementation begins not with a roadmap, but with an honest reading of the organisation as it actually behaves under pressure.
