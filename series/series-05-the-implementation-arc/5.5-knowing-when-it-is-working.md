# 5.5 — Knowing When It Is Working

The Pulse is not a dashboard. It is a diagnostic.

This article establishes the operational measurement model for a functioning architectural decision system.

The measurement model focuses on:

- Decision Latency
- Deviation Rate
- Rework Ratio
- The Dual Heartbeat
- Architectural Decay
- Refinement cadence

The goal is not governance activity. The goal is visible leverage under delivery pressure.
