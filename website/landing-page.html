The sequence

Most EA frameworks are sequenced incorrectly.

The standard sequence runs: produce artefacts → have conversations → make decisions. The sequence that works in practice runs: have the decision conversation → identify what needs to be recorded → produce the minimum artefact that captures the commitment. VAF operationalises the correct sequence.

Standard sequence:
produce artefacts
→ have conversations
→ make decisions

VAF sequence:
have the decision conversation
→ identify what to record
→ produce minimum artefact
→ record the commitment

Time saved: 70–90%
per artefact
