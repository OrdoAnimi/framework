# AI Advisory Launch Structure

## Status

Internal launch preparation. Do not publish externally until the governance model, readiness model, and offer boundaries have been reviewed.

## Commercial Structure

Use one entity and one commercial front door.

ZenCloud Advisory remains the entity-facing advisory wrapper.

Velocity Architecture provides the method and governance backbone.

StudioSix supports production, packaging, and delivery presentation.

Magister Automatorum remains an incubator and proof-of-work stream for resource-aware AI operations.

## Initial Service Line

**AI Architecture and Governance Advisory**

Positioning:

ZenCloud helps organisations apply the lessons learned from cloud governance to AI deployment: decision ownership, data boundaries, tool governance, human review, cost control, and operating model design.

## Initial Offers

### AI Architecture Readiness Sprint

A short assessment of business alignment, architecture readiness, data boundaries, model/tool choices, security, human accountability, delivery path, and AI FinOps.

### AI Governance Operating Model Review

A review of roles, decision rights, policies, review gates, risk controls, evidence requirements, and operational ownership.

### Agent and Workflow Control Assessment

A focused review of tool permissions, read/write boundaries, approval gates, failure handling, logging, and human review for agentic workflows.

## Buyer Problems

- Tools and subscriptions are duplicating across teams.
- Data boundaries are unclear.
- Vendors are shaping architecture decisions.
- Human accountability is weak.
- Costs are rising before value is measured.
- Pilots have no controlled production path.
