# AI Advisory Engagement Pack

## Purpose

Define a repeatable, bounded advisory engagement for organisations moving from AI interest or pilots toward governed deployment.

## Offer

### AI Architecture Readiness Sprint

A short advisory engagement covering business alignment, architecture readiness, data boundaries, model and tool choices, security, human accountability, delivery path, and AI FinOps.

## Typical Duration

Two to four weeks, depending on stakeholder access, use-case complexity, and evidence availability.

## Inputs

- priority AI use cases
- current architecture and data context
- existing AI, cloud, security, and data policies
- current tool and vendor estate
- pilot documentation
- cost and usage information where available
- stakeholder interviews

## Activities

1. Executive and stakeholder intake
2. Use-case classification
3. Architecture and dependency review
4. Data-boundary review
5. Model and tool option review
6. Human accountability and agent-control review
7. FinRev DevOps assessment
8. Risk and control review
9. Readiness scoring
10. Executive playback and action plan

## Deliverables

- AI use-case canvases
- AI deployment readiness assessment
- architecture decision backlog
- data-boundary findings
- model and tool selection considerations
- human-review and accountability model
- AI risk and control register
- FinRev DevOps findings
- 30/60/90-day action plan
- executive summary

## Engagement Boundaries

Included:

- architecture and governance advisory
- readiness assessment
- decision framing
- operating-model design
- risk and control definition
- deployment-path guidance

Not included unless separately agreed:

- foundation-model training
- deep ML engineering
- production coding
- penetration testing
- legal or regulatory advice
- managed operations
- vendor procurement execution

## Decision Outcomes

The engagement should conclude with one of four outcomes:

- proceed to controlled pilot
- proceed with conditions
- return to architecture framing
- stop or defer

## Success Measures

- clear ownership
- explicit data boundaries
- agreed human-review controls
- prioritised architecture decisions
- realistic cost and value model
- controlled pilot or deployment path

## Follow-On Options

- AI Governance Operating Model Review
- Agent and Workflow Control Assessment
- architecture decision sprint
- deployment governance support
- operating-review cadence
